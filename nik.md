# 🎓 **PROJECT TITLE**

> **Cognitive Multi-Agent AI Academic Assistant with Adaptive Learning**

---

# 🧠 **1. PROJECT DESCRIPTION (What Your System Is)**

The **Cognitive Multi-Agent AI Academic Assistant** is an intelligent academic support system designed to assist students in understanding, analyzing, and interacting with their study materials.

Unlike traditional learning tools, which are static and passive, this system uses **multiple cooperating AI agents** to simulate tutor-like intelligence, enabling adaptive explanations, automated assessments, performance analysis, and intelligent study planning.

The system integrates **local and cloud-based open-source language models** to provide context-aware responses grounded in user-provided academic content.
